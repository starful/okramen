About OKRamen

🍜 What this site is

OKRamen is a free, bilingual trip-planning site for Japan ramen. We help travelers shortlist shops on a map, skim style explainers, and open per-shop pages with practical notes (area, soup style, what to order, Maps link) before visiting.

Important: We are not the official website of any ramen restaurant listed here. Hours, menus, prices, and wait times change — always confirm on Google Maps or at the shop.

🗺️ What you get

  • Interactive map — filter by soup style (tonkotsu, shoyu, miso, shio, tsukemen, and more).
  • Shop guides — each page leads with a quick visit box, then optional background notes.
  • Editorial guides — etiquette, regional styles, toppings, and travel tips in English and Korean.

✍️ How we create content

Shop pages combine public location data, common travel knowledge, and editorial writing. Lead images are AI-generated illustrations for layout only — they may not match real interiors or dishes. We improve pages over time based on search feedback and user reports.

🔍 Editorial standards

  • No claim that we rank shops as “the best in Japan” on every page — tastes differ.
  • Practical information (Maps, style, ordering tips) comes before long background text.
  • We link to our Privacy Policy for analytics and advertising partners.

📬 Contact & corrections

Spotted wrong coordinates, a closed shop, or a shop we should add? Use our contact form — we read every message.
