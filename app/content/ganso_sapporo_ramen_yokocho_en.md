Ganso Sapporo Ramen Yokocho (元祖さっぽろラーメン横丁) is a short alley in **Susukino**, Sapporo’s main nightlife area. It is not one restaurant: you choose among **many small counters**, each serving its own take on **Sapporo miso ramen** — usually wok-fried miso tare, thick curly noodles, and toppings such as butter and sweet corn.

**Practical notes:** Seats are limited (often counter-only), so expect a short wait at dinner and late night. Many travelers visit after 10 p.m.; winter evenings are busy. There is no single “house bowl” for the whole alley — walk the lane, read each shop’s menu board, and order at that stall’s ticket machine or counter. Hours and holidays differ **per shop**; use the Maps link above and verify on site.

**Area:** Susukino is walkable from central Sapporo stations with hotels and bars nearby. Combine with other Susukino food stops or a daytime walk to Odori / Sapporo Station after your bowl.
